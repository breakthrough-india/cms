State chapters come together to showcase the richness of India's regional cultures. Expect live performances, art installations, food stalls, and interactive craft workshops.

## Highlights

- Performances by folk artists from 12 state chapters
- Craft market featuring traditional artisans
- Regional cuisine pavilion
- Interactive storytelling sessions for children

This is a free, family-friendly event. No registration required to attend; register if you wish to volunteer or exhibit.
