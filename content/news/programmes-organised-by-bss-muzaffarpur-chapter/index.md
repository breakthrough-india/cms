The Breakthrough Science Society Muzaffarpur Chapter organised two programmes on 29th  May, 2025 at D. N. High School in the city and at Saraiya. The topic of Discussion at D. N. School was “Science and Superstition”. At Saraiya M. K. Academy, the topic was ‘Climate Change’.  Mr. Chanchal Ghosh, All India Secretariat member of BSS, and Mr. Ashutosh Kumar, In-charge of BSS Muzaffarpur chapter conducted the discussions.
