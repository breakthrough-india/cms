Science Learning Centre in Chhattisgarh ( Koriya district )

Science Learning Centre was inaugurated on April 9, 2025, at the Government Girls’ Secondary School, Charcha, Koriya district. The event was organized by the entire school staff in association with the Breakthrough Science Society, Koriya chapter. The state in-charge of BSS, Ms. Pooja Sharma, was the main speaker. The event was graced by the esteemed presence of the District Education Officer, Mr. Jitendra Gupta, along with respected Mr. Bhatt, Mr. Bajpai, and Mr. Tiwari from the district office. Principals from both clusters of Charcha, school staff and students were present. The curriculum for the year-long activities of the Science Learning Centre was unveiled during the program.
