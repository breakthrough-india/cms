On May 20, 2025, a memorial meeting in honour of Prof. Jayant Narlikar was organized by the Universe Science Forum in Ahmedabad. The meeting began with the reading of the tribute message from the Breakthrough Science Society. Prof. Mitul Makwana, Mr.Prashantbhai Joshi, Mr.K.R. Chaudhary, Mr.Niranjanbhai Shah, Mr.Binitbhai Modi, Mr.Manishbhai Sonara, Mr.Ashwinbhai Prajapati, Mr.Kishan Malviya, Mr.Vaibhav Trivedi, Mr.Abhay Patel, Mr.Dilip Satashiya, and several students paid heartfelt tributes to Prof.Narlikar. Mr.Prashantbhai Joshi, an advisor to the Universe Science Forum, highlighted Prof. Narlikar’s commitment to the scientific approach, his significant role in proving through experiments that astrology is not a science and his popular book “Brahmand Series”. Mr.Dinesh Mohanta, a secretariat member of the Breakthrough Science Society, spoke about Prof. Narlikar’s emphasis on learning science through simple experiments and his efforts to cultivate a scientific mindset. Everyone expressed their resolve to draw inspiration from Prof. Narlikar’s life and ideas and to incorporate them into their own lives.
